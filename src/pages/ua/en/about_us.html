Mobility programs for students and teachers

Erasmus+KA1 program

What it is?

The Erasmus+KA1 direction envisages cooperation for the mutual exchange of students and employees of higher education institutions based on the conclusion of bilateral inter-institutional agreements between higher education institutions of program member countries and partner countries
